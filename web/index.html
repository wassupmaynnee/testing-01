    FAQ

    Questions, answered

    How long can my source video be?

    Anything from a 30-second take to a multi-hour podcast or stream. Longer videos use more credits because you get more clips out of them — the engine scans the whole transcript for the strongest moments.

    Do I need a GPU or special hardware?

    No. Everything renders in the cloud on standard CPUs. If you can open a browser tab, you can ship clips.

    What exactly do I download?

    Vertical 9:16 H.264 MP4s at 1080×1920 with captions burned in — the exact file TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts want. No re-encoding, no editor pass.

    How do credits work?

    One credit = one finished clip. The Free tier includes a one-time 30-credit trial (no credit card). Paid plans refresh their allowance every month, billed monthly or annually — your choice.

    Can I adjust the captions and framing?

    Captions are word-timed and styled automatically, and face tracking keeps the speaker centered in the crop. You can fine-tune wording, caption style, and framing before exporting.

    Which languages are supported?

    Transcription works across dozens of spoken languages, and captions come out in the language spoken in your video.
